FAQ

Quick answers to common questions.

What is a scenepack?

A scenepack is a collection of high quality clips of a specific character, show or movie — trimmed and ready for video editors to use in edits, AMVs and montages.

Are the downloads free?

Yes — every pack on Solar Scenepacks is completely free to download. No sign-ups, no paywalls.

What quality are the packs?

Most packs are 1080p or 4K, cut scene-by-scene so you only get clean, usable clips without intros or credits.

How do I request a scenepack?

Click Request a Pack in the menu — it takes you to our Discord where you can drop your request. Popular requests get made first.

Can I use these clips in my edits?

Yes, that's what they're for! Packs are fan-made for entertainment and editing practice. If you post your edit, crediting the site or Discord is appreciated but not required.
